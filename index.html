Why Siricoin and not any other crypto?

Main features of Siricoin

Free and Open-Source

It's free and open source so you can contribute to it on Github.

Decentralized

Siricoin is decentralized meaning it can be mined all over the world without issues or disconnection.

Light Weight

Siricoin is lightweight hence it can be mined on microcontrollers.

Profitable

Since the coin is mineable on microcontrollers there's a variable difficulty, however the more hash you have the more you get ;)

About Us

The short birth story of Siricoin

We - Siricoin devs first met up on a crypto currency discord server and we didnt know much about crypto at the time and didnt know how to code ¯\_(ツ)_/¯ one fine day we all got banned from that discord server, from there onwards we took matters into our own hands and decided to make our own crypto currency.

Since we didn't know how to code and make cryptos we looking up different cryptos that can be mined on android phones and avrs, but we couldnt find any of them which were decentralized. So we learnt Coding and came to a final decision of making Siricoin which can be mined on low powered devices :)
